Detailed Description

Represents a physical particle in simulations, encompassing properties such as position, velocity, and mass.

This class is designed to simulate the dynamics of a particle within a physical system, accounting for its kinematic properties (position and velocity) and dynamic properties (mass, density, and pressure).

◆ update()

void models::Particle::update ( const double dt)
inline

Updates the particle's state based on its acceleration and the time step.

Parameters
dtThe time step size used for the update.

This method advances the particle's position and velocity according to Newton's second law of motion. Acceleration affects the velocity, which in turn affects the position. After updating the dynamics, the acceleration is reset, assuming a constant force model where forces are recalculated at each time step.
